Ingredients

  • Spagget Noodles
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook noods til al dente
  2. Slice garlic to chips and saute them till golden brown in 3 tbsp of olive oil
  3. drain noods and mix in olive oil, garlic chips, about 1 tbsp of both salt and pepper, and a tsp of Crushed red pepper
  4. TASTE IT and spice accordingly
  5. serve topped with some parm
